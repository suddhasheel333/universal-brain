Tickets with confidence ≥ 0.45 are marked AUTOMATED — the system resolves them directly. Below that threshold, they go to MANUAL REVIEW. A noise gate at 0.25 raw similarity prevents garbage matches from surfacing at all. High-confidence critical tickets fire an SNS alert to the infrastructure team.
